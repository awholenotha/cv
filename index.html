Nikko Nelson

Filmmaker & Storyteller who goes by awholenotha

Hey, my name’s Nikko (i.e. awholenotha). I’m a screenwriter/director based out of College Park, Georgia that loves creating and sharing engaging stories about the human experience. I believe through written and visual expression the potential to realize our individual journeys as connected and tangible experiences matter greatly. Sharing stories inspires hope, understanding, and change within others and ultimately the world. To be aware of this power strengthens the choices we make, and I choose to use my power to emphasize the need for empathy within our world through media. (I also have a B.A. in Film/Video Production & dual African-American Studies/Theatre Minors from Georgia State University since those things matter.)
